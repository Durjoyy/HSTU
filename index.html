About HSTU

Hajee Mohammad Danesh Science and Technology University (HSTU) is a government-financed Public University of Bangladesh.Hajee Mohammad Danesh Science & Technology University is the first Science and Technology University in the northern region & The second oldest Science and Technology University of Bangladesh. Locally it is known as Hajee Danesh University.
